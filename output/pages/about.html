About

George is a professional journalist, part-time coder and novice photographer currently based in Washington, D.C.

His work includes time at Atlantic Media's Defense One, MuckRock (not Muck Rack), The Salem News, and coffee shops and his home as a freelance reporter working mostly for the Boston Globe and its holdings.

He hails from Central Massachusetts, not far form where the Squannacook and Nashua rivers meet. Send George an email or a tweet @rhymeswthgeorge.
